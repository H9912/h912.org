~Aria~

Aria is an pre-apocalyptic RPG where the main character, Eleie, is escaping the corruption to survive in a collapsing world. She will meet friends along the way and will have to take care of them, and discover their secrets. You are the master of her destiny, and your choices will define the story and Eleie's friends. For now the game is still in "brainstorming" phase. We write all of our ideas and designing all the characters. As we are slightly better at drawing than coding, the game itself takes time to develop cause we are learning everything at the same time. I'm not alone on this, we are a small team of 4 working hard on it! Artymett is writing and drawing super-emotional scenes, Walitam is our programmer, and Rea is making the environment of the universe and giving TON of great ideas.
