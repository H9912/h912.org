~Aria~

Aria is an pre-apocalyptic RPG where the main character, Eleie, is escaping the corruption to survive in a collapsing world. She will meet friends along the way and will have to take care of them, and discover their secrets. You are the master of her destiny, and your choices will define the story, aswell as Eleie's friends. For now the game is still in "brainstorming" phase. We write all of our ideas and designing all the characters. As we are slightly better at drawing than coding, the game itself takes time to make. Also, we are learning everything by ourself. We're a team of 4 talented people : Artymett, Walitam, me, and Rea. I'll keep this page updated as we progress in the development.
